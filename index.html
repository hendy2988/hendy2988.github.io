Let’s get started!

Finding A Job

When you are looking for a job, the best place to start is to literally start looking for a job! When seeking for employment look at yourself, what are your interests? What do you enjoy doing? Looking at yourself and finding what you can do and what you are capable of is important in looking for a job. After you analyze yourself, and have a good understanding in what you are wanting to do, start looking for openings in job fields that match you. Where to start looking varies on many variables, one variable is looking at the newspaper. The newspaper is a great resource and is a key variable, but don't give up when you can't find an opening in the paper. There are many more resources to keep looking! Always keep a look out, talk to community members, bulletin boards, always keep a sharp eye out! The internet is a very important resource this day in age, so make sure you scope out the internet too! For more tips and information, click up to the Finding A Job link!


The Resume

A resume is a brief document condensing your various accomplishments and abilities and presenting them in their best light to your potential employers! This will be the main physical record your bosses will refer to as a testament to your capabilities! A resume is generally sent to your potential employers prior to an interview, so that they can decide whether or not the company can use you in its system. In this way, the resume is a key factor in determining whether or not you receive a callback for an interview, and can even help your employers make a final decision regarding your employment after the interview! For more information regarding your resume, click the Resume link!


The Application

The Application is the place where everything comes together! The lights, music and action! The application is a form that is filled out by you, you fill out all of your personal information, your schooling, previous experience, the list goes on! Filling out the application is a very important part, in showing your future employer what you are capable of! For more tips and information, click the Application link!


The Interview

The Interview is the final step of the job application process! It's one of, if not the, most important steps in the series, and for many it's also the most nerve-racking. However, we have listed our own set of tips and tricks designed to put you at ease in an interview setting as well as really help you impress your potential employers! We've even written up a short summary of important details to remember when preparing for your interview so that you can really feel confident and successful walking into that interview setting! For more information, click the Interview link!
